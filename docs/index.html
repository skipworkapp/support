A local-first data toolkit. Import, merge, and review spreadsheets entirely on your device — nothing is sent to a server.

What it does

  • Table merger — join two CSV, XLSX, or JSON tables on matching fields and export the result.
  • Match review — step through subjects and pick the correct match for each.
  • Flexible import — CSV, XLSX, and JSON/JSONL through a guided import wizard.
  • Local-first — your files stay on your device; nothing is uploaded.
  • App lock — optional biometric or PIN lock with auto-lock after backgrounding.
  • Handoff & export — continue a session across devices and save results as XLSX.
